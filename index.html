About Me

Who am I?

As you've probably guessed, my name is Carter. I've been living in Nashville since 2011

When I'm not practicing writing code or working on projects I spend my time taking my motorcycle "Tuesday" out and finding a nice backroad to lose myself. If I'm not riding Tuesday I'm typically learning about or appreciating beautiful automotive creations.

During my adult life in the Nashville area I have spent most of my time working in the automotive industry. I have decided to leave that industry because I realized an important part of living a happy life is to not mix work and play.

If I had infinite funds and never had to work another day in my life I would buy a 1985 m635csi and spend almost every waking moment driving through the swiss alps.
